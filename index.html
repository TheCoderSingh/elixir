About Elixir

Elixir is a cultural, technical and an entrepreneurial fest organized by the students of Apeejay College of Fine Arts, Jalandhar. It is a fest where both school and college students can showcase their talent. It is an annual event of Apeejay College of Fine Arts, Jalandhar and is being organized for the first time. It consists of workshops such as Ethical Hacking, Robotics, Internet of Things (IoT) and Digital Marketing and competitions like Business Plan, RoboSoccer, LAN Gaming, Dance, On The Spot Painting, Ad Mad Show and many more...
